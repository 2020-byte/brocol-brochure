TEAM

We are a team of students from UBC Okanagan, working to create a transformative tool with Brokol App that addresses food waste in our community. Driven by our shared goal of fostering sustainable consumption, we are committed to make a significant, positive impact on global food waste reduction. Join us in our journey as we strive to empower individuals towards more mindful grocery habits, paving the way for a more sustainable future for all.
